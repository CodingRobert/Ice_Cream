Ice Cream Menu

Classic Vanilla

Savor the classic, creamy delight of our Vanilla Ice Cream, made with pure vanilla beans and fresh cream for an irresistibly smooth texture. Each scoop melts gently on your tongue, offering a perfect balance of rich, sweet vanilla flavor and velvety softness. Crafted with simple, high-quality ingredients, this timeless treat brings comfort and joy in every bite. Whether enjoyed alone or paired with your favorite dessert, our Vanilla Ice Cream is a refreshing indulgence that satisfies cravings and brightens any moment. Experience the pure essence of vanilla in every creamy spoonful.

Pistachio

Discover the nutty elegance of our Pistachio Ice Cream, where creamy richness meets the distinct, subtly sweet flavor of roasted pistachios. Made with premium pistachio nuts and fresh cream, this ice cream boasts a smooth, luxurious texture with just the right amount of natural crunch. Its delicate green hue invites you to enjoy a sophisticated treat that’s both refreshing and indulgent. Perfect for those seeking a unique, flavorful dessert, our Pistachio Ice Cream offers a harmonious blend of creamy sweetness and earthy nuttiness in every spoonful. A true delight for nut lovers.

Chocolate

Indulge in the rich, velvety goodness of our Chocolate Ice Cream — a timeless favorite made with premium cocoa and fresh cream. Each spoonful offers a deep, satisfying chocolate flavor with a luxuriously smooth texture that melts in your mouth. Crafted in small batches to preserve its intense taste and creamy consistency, it’s the ultimate treat for true chocolate lovers. Whether enjoyed alone or paired with other desserts, this classic flavor never fails to delight. Pure, bold, and perfectly sweet, our Chocolate Ice Cream is a comforting and indulgent escape in every bite.

Nutella

Treat yourself to the irresistible taste of our Nutella Ice Cream, where creamy hazelnut spread meets rich chocolate in a dreamy frozen dessert. Made with real Nutella and fresh ingredients, this smooth and decadent ice cream delivers the beloved hazelnut-chocolate flavor you know and love in every spoonful. Its silky texture and nutty sweetness make it a standout choice for those craving something uniquely indulgent. Swirled with ribbons of Nutella for an extra touch of delight, this flavor is pure bliss — perfect for chocolate lovers, hazelnut fans, or anyone who appreciates a luxurious ice cream experience.

Lemon

Refresh your palate with our bright and tangy Lemon Ice Cream, a zesty twist on traditional frozen treats. Made with real lemon juice and zest, it offers a naturally fresh flavor with just the right balance of sweetness and citrus. Light, creamy, and perfectly smooth, this ice cream is ideal for warm days or as a refreshing finish to any meal. Its clean, uplifting taste awakens the senses and leaves a cool, invigorating impression. Simple yet sophisticated, our Lemon Ice Cream brings a touch of sunshine to every scoop — a truly revitalizing dessert experience.
